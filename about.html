Our Story

Where Luxury
Meets Trust

Founded in 2009 by automotive enthusiasts with a shared vision, Lupin Motors began as a small boutique showroom in downtown Manhattan. What started with just 12 hand-selected vehicles has grown into one of the region's most respected premium dealerships.

Our philosophy has never changed: every vehicle that bears our name must meet the highest standards of quality, performance, and provenance. We don't just sell cars — we curate experiences for drivers who demand the very best.
