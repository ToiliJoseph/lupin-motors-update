Common Questions

Financing FAQ

What do I need to apply for auto financing?

You'll need a valid driver's license, proof of income (pay stubs or tax returns), proof of residence, and your Social Security number. If you're trading in a vehicle, bring the title and registration. Pre-approval takes just minutes online or at our dealership.

Can I get approved with bad credit?

Absolutely. We work with specialized lenders who focus on helping customers rebuild their credit. Our finance team has experience securing approvals for all credit situations. We recommend getting pre-qualified first so you know your budget before shopping.

How long does the approval process take?

Most customers receive a decision within 15–20 minutes during business hours. For complex applications or after-hours submissions, you may receive a response by the next business day. Our online pre-qualification tool gives instant results without affecting your credit score.

Can I pay off my loan early without penalties?

Yes! All of our lending partners offer loans with no prepayment penalties. You can pay off your loan in full at any time or make extra payments to reduce your principal faster. This can save you significant money on interest over the life of the loan.

Do you offer leasing options?

Yes, we offer competitive lease programs on most new vehicles. Leasing typically requires lower monthly payments and a smaller down payment than financing. At the end of your lease term, you can purchase the vehicle, lease a new one, or simply return it. Ask our finance team about current lease specials.

What is gap insurance and do I need it?

Gap insurance covers the difference between what you owe on your loan and what your insurance pays if your vehicle is totaled or stolen. If you put less than 20% down or finance for 60+ months, we strongly recommend gap insurance. It can be added to your loan for just a few dollars per month.
